ABOUT.SH

Hello! I'm Javier, I'm a creative strategist, educator, and co-founder of Happy Punk Panda, a digital agency helping Central American brands find their voice (and edge) in the digital world. With over 15 years of experience, I’ve worked across content, branding, and strategy — often at the intersection of culture and tech.

My background is in Multimedia Design, and I’ve spent just as much time in classrooms as I have in boardrooms. As a long-time teacher at the Escuela de Creatividad Estratégica Mónica Herrera, I’ve guided students through the shifting media landscape, always encouraging imagination, ethics, and experimentation.

For some weird reason, I also chose to be a runner. And when I’m not at work or on the road, I’m probably breaking (and rebuilding) my smart home setup.

INIT_BLOG.SH

I’ve spent most of my professional life making things for other people — brands, strategies, content, endless decks. And that’s great. I like building things that work.

But I wanted a place where I could just write. No client. No brief. No algorithm. Just somewhere to think out loud.

This blog isn’t meant to be perfect or even consistent. It’s a mix of thoughts around creativity, internet culture, communication, and the stuff I keep coming back to — sometimes from work, sometimes from teaching, sometimes just from curiosity.

I’m not trying to grow an audience or launch a newsletter. I just missed having a corner of the internet that was mine. I missed playing in the internet without having to have a goal.

So here it is.
